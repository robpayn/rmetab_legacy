Eco-Agent resource.water Definition Package

Overview

The water package defines fluxing and storing for all water connections in all types of compartments. Currently, the compartment types include surface (channel) and ground water. The next compartment type to be added will be atmosphere.

Originally, the ground water storage compartment was called simply "ground" but as the heat package was added, it became necessary to separate the ground compartments into the water-holding portion and non-water-holding portions, or pore-space and sediment, respectively. The pore-space portion is now called gndwater while the non-pore-space portion is called sediment.
